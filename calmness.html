A Canvas of Calmness

Human eyes are highly adapted to discern the nuances of green, making it the most restful color for us to view. Because it sits at the center of the visible color spectrum, green requires no adjustment from the eye, providing an immediate sense of rest and relaxation.

Psychologically, soft and muted greens—like sage, mint, and pale olive—lower our heart rates and reduce anxiety. It is the color of sanctuary. Whether it is a quiet garden or a softly painted room, green creates a peaceful environment that promotes balance, harmony, and emotional stability.
